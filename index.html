Easy Peasy Eats

8 oz spaghetti 3 tbsp unsalted butter 4 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt to taste Black pepper to taste 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional) 1 tbsp olive oil
Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente; drain and set aside.
